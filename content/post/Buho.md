BuhoCMS is a local Content Management System for static site generators.

See BuhoCMS as a GUI for static site generators. Easily create or open a website, choose your theme and add your first posts. With BuhoCMS, adding and editing content is as easy as a few clicks. No more terminal commands, folder hunting or manual Front matter edits 

![Buho](/posts/buho.png)

It's available on Flathub under POP OS so it just really needs Hugo installed and it's pretty much good to go. There are a few alpha bugs - "Themes" options is not quite working so you are better off setting up the nuts and bolts of the site under Hugo and then adding your chosen theme as a submodule. Hugo do a simple tutorial [here](https://gohugo.io/getting-started/quick-start/)
